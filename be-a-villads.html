Be a Villads

So you want to become a member of The Villads Society? Good news! We are actively recruiting new members that fit our carefully designed requirements. Our primary and only requirement is that your legal name must be Villads (which by the way is the only correct spelling of the name). This might sound like an easy requirement to pass, although we warn you that being a Villads comes with a huge responsibility. People not only expect you to catch pigeons, but you're also expected to live in accordance with the Urban Dictionary description of Villads: A word to describe the most awesome kind of people if you are a Villads then you are just perfect.
