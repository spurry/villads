Diversity Statement

The Villads Society is committed to uphold and encourage a space of diversity and inclusion. Diversity is an integral part of furthering our mission. We will only be able to grow as a society with a diverse group of members. Fostering an environment of equality is something we strive to achieve.

Therefore, we do not discriminate against any member or potential member of the society based on: race, religion, sex, age, sexual orientation, marital status, disability, shoe size, hair color, pet ownership, vehicle of choice, music taste, food preferences or veteran status.
